Christian Howes and Daniel Kelly have developed a distinctive sound and versatile repertoire through performing more than 500 engagements including concerts, festivals and corporate events throughtout the Midwest.

Inspiring audiences with their dynamic performances, they articulate a wide range of moods and emotions by fusing elements of jazz. classical and world music. Individually, they have toured and recorded extensively in various musical ensembles throughtout Europe and the United States.
