Christian howes

 

 

Christian Howes has received numerous acclaims for his unique contributions to the largely unforged field of Jazz Violin. Classically trained since the age of five, Christian appeared as a guest soloist with the Columbus Symphony Orchestra at Sixteen.

He recently produced his second album, Ten Yard, featuring nine original compositions, which the L.A. Times review as "exceptional... Howes is a jazz player whose solid, classically trained technique provides the freedon to fully express an expansive improvisaional imagination" (Don Heckman, The Los Angeles Times, June 19, 1998).

He recently made his New York debut at Birdland; look for this performance on the RCA Victor recordings, Live at Birdland.
